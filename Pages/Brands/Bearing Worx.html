About Bearing Worx

Bearing Worx is a progressive new brand imported and distributed throughout Australia exclusively by Link International.

Bearing Worx offers a wide range of bearing kits all at affordable prices. Each kit consists of each part needed to recondition that particular part of the bike and has individually packed compartments for easy application.

To find the right bearings for your bike, please use the Find Your Parts search above.
