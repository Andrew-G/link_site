Vee Rubber Tyres

General Technical Info

When fitting new tyres:

  • Correct fitting and balancing by a trained technician is essential.
  • Check for wheel alignment.
  • New tyres require a ‘Run In’ procedure to enable the tread surface to provide correct performance.
  • When fitting tubes, use Talcum powder to prevent tube sticking.
  • Regularly check tyre inflation pressure.

Tyre Identification

The Tyre Identification System gives information on:

  • Tyre Width (Inch or mm)
  • Aspect Ratio - Height / Width X 100
  • Rim Diameter
  • Maximum Speed Rating (Alpha)
  • Tyre Load Index (Numeric)

Example: 120 / 90 – 18 70H
Tyre Width = 120 mm
Aspect Ratio = 90%
Rim Diameter = 18 inch
Load Index = 70 (355 kg)
Speed Rating = H (210 Km/h)
