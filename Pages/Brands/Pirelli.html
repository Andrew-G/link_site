Pirelli Tyres

Welcome to the official site for Pirelli Motorcycle Tyres in Australia.

Pirelli is one of the biggest tyre manufacturers in the world. Pirelli has been producing tyres for over 100 years and is well known in the motorcycling world for their technical precision and their advancements in cutting-edge motorcycle tyre technology.

The Pirelli range caters for all segments of the motorcycling market with performance tyres suited to motocross, enduro, trials, scooter, cruiser, commuter, touring, sport touring, sports and racing bike applications. These include the Number 1 for mileage Angel GT, Scorpion MX Extra X and Diablo Supercorsa SP tyres, plus the Night Dragon for cruiser motorcycles.

As a reflection of their quality and high performance, Pirelli is the Original Equipment Supplier for a number of prestigious bike manufacturers such as Aprilia, Ducati, Honda, BMW, KTM and Husqvarna. Pirelli is also the exclusive supplier of tyres for the World Superbike Championship now extended through to 2018.

Pirelli has a long history of outstanding success in a range of racing disciplines. As recent as 2014, for example, Pirelli Scorpion MX riders have won the premier World MX1 World Championship and also MX2 World Championships which brings their MX World Championships up to an impressive 62 titles. In Australia Pirelli has taken out the MX Nationals Open Championship and Super X Championship double with Matt Moss in 2013 and 2014, as well as winning the MX Nationals Pro Lites with Luke Clout in 2014. Glenn Allerton has also won the 2014 Australian Superbike Championship using Pirelli Diablo Superbike tyres. 2015 sees the launch of the much anticipated Scorpion Trail 2 tyre for adventure motorcycle riders in Australia. Pirelli is also Official Tyre of the MX Nationals, BEARS Racing, Pirelli Road Race Series, and Motorcycle Sportsmen of QLD and is homologated for competition in the 2015 Australian Superbike Championship.
