Cobra Exhaust Systems

For three decades COBRA has understood the motorcycling dream. The dream to do something to your bike that will make it stand out from the crowd – an exhaust to be visually pleasing, sound awesome and yet most importantly still deliver great performance.

Cobra specializes in producing “dream” exhausts for both Japanese Cruisers and Harley Davidson bikes. To help you get the most from your new COBRA Exhaust System they have a full complement of Electronic Fuel Management systems and Carburetor Jet Kits available.

COBRA also manufacture a range of other cruiser accessories, namely Sissy Bars and Racks, Freeway Bars, Floorboards, Light bar Kits, Saddle Bag Supports, Tachometers and much more.

COBRA products are all manufactured in the US, making their dream possible in continuing to give you the highest quality product possible.
