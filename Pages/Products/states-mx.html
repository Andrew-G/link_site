States MX

States MX is a new and innovative brand released onto the market in 2012. It is owned and trademarked exclusively by Link International. This product line is primarily made up of alloy hard parts anodized in many different colours to add that finishing touch to your dirt bike.

These products are the result of constant and careful analysis paying particular attention to the present and future needs of all types of race and recreational motorcycle enthusiasts. Using the latest computer designed and controlled metal cutting equipment, the stringent controls of the R&D Engineers and specialized technicians, States MX ensures high quality production that keeps it at the market forefront.

The extensive range of off road parts includes but is not limited to items such as the Hybrid steel/alloy combination sprocket, bar ends, case savers, foot pegs, levers, hour meters, launch controls, clutch lever and perch assemblies, front brake master cylinder rotator clamps.
