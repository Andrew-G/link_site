General Questions

  • Can I buy products directly from your warehouse/website?
    Link International is an Importer / Distributor ONLY and will not sell directly to the public. We encourage you to visit your local dealer and order our prodcuts through them.

    If you would like to make this process as easy as possible, find the parts you want via our Product range, Parts Search or Make/Model search functions and add them to your Wishlist. You can then print your wishlist off and take it into your local motorcycle dealer.
