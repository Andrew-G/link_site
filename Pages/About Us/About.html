About Us

Link International started in 1987, supplying quality imported motoring parts, accessories and service for nearly 30 years. We are a continuous link between high end overseas suppliers and Australian retail outlets: providing the know-how and back-up essential when selling quality products.

We are known throughout the industry as suppliers of motoring essentials and represent some of the world's top brands. Link's product range includes Motul Motorcycle Oil and Maintenance Products, Pirelli Motorcycle Tyres, RK Takasago Chains, Arrow Exhausts, Ferodo Brake Pads, Polisport Plastics, Ariete Grips and Accessories, BMC Air Filters, Vortex Racing Products plus many more as well as a line of related workshop products.

With close to 100 staff across Australia, Link International is one of the country's premium importers and wholesale distributors of quality motorcycle parts and accessories. The company is recognised as a market leader in terms of the variety of media advertising and external promotions it undertakes. Link International's marketing strategy is designed to support its suppliers and dealers by providing the largest possible exposure for its premium product range and therefore help generate the greatest amount of customer interest.

Our product management system is designed to keep our brands ahead of the competition; and more importantly, to ensure that we keep the promises we make to both our suppliers, clients and customers.
