Distribution Network

Link International's distribution network covers 99 percent of the retail motorcycle outlets in Australia. The products we represent are the industry essentials and we handle more than 2000 retail outlets throughout Australia.

Link International offers Australia-wide distribution of parts and accessories through an effective sales force of extremely qualified, professional representatives and locally based state agents (when logistics dictate it). This gives us maximum coverage of the motorcycle industry.

Link has access to warehouses in Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth and Launceston either by ownership or through agencies. Link employs a large team in the distribution hubs to ensure shipments of products are received and distributed efficiently.
