Careers at Link International

Link International is a vibrant and rapidly growing Importer and Distributor of quality motorcycle parts and accessories with offices situated throughout Australia. Our Head Office location is in Brisbane and offers a wide range of opportunities for perspective employees.

The Link International environment offers a chance to work with high-quality products and globally recognized brands. You will work with a diverse and dedicated group of skilled professionals and be an integral member of the Link International team.

Link International's Domestic and International Business profile brings exciting and unique career opportunities in the following areas: design, finance, account management, information systems, distribution, marketing, research and development, purchasing and sales.

If you have a passion for motorcycles or are particularly adept at your chosen profession we would love to hear from you.
